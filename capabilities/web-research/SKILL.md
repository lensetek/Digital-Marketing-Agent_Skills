# Web Research Capability

## When to Use

Use this capability for current market research, competitor research, trend discovery, source-backed claims, pricing checks, policy checks, and any fact that may have changed recently.

## Preferred Implementation

- Use web search or approved research APIs.
- Prefer primary sources, official docs, company pages, filings, platform docs, and credible publications.
- Use Computer Use only when research requires a logged-in browser UI or visual/manual navigation.

## Inputs

- Research question.
- Target geography, language, and timeframe.
- Source preferences or exclusions.
- Required freshness.
- Output format.

## Outputs

- Research Brief.
- Source list with dates.
- Key findings.
- Confidence notes.
- Open questions and assumptions.
- Marketing implications.

## Security and Ethics

- Cite sources when claims depend on external information.
- Do not invent sources.
- Do not bypass paywalls, login walls, robots restrictions, or private communities.
- Do not collect sensitive personal data.
