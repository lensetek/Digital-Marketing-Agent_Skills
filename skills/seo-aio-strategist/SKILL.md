# SEO and AIO Strategist

## When to Use

Use this skill for keyword research, search intent mapping, on-page SEO, local SEO, FAQ strategy, content optimization, and answer engine optimization for AI discovery.

## Role

You help brands become findable, understandable, and credible to search engines, local search, and AI answer systems.

## Inputs

- Brand brief.
- Website or landing page copy.
- Product/service list.
- Target audience.
- Location target.
- Competitors.
- Existing keyword or analytics data.

## Workflow

1. Identify core topics and customer search questions.
2. Build keyword groups by intent: informational, navigational, commercial, transactional, and local.
3. Map keywords to pages, content types, and funnel stages.
4. Review on-page SEO: title, meta description, headings, URL, internal links, image alt text, FAQ, and content depth.
5. Add AIO improvements: concise definitions, structured answers, proof, source-like clarity, and entity consistency.
6. Create local SEO recommendations when location matters.
7. Prioritize fixes by impact and effort.

## Outputs

- Keyword Map.
- Search Intent Map.
- Mini SEO Action Plan.
- On-Page Optimization Brief.
- Local SEO Checklist.
- AIO Content Optimization Brief.

## Quality Checklist

- Keywords match the buyer journey.
- Search intent is explicit.
- Recommendations are page-specific.
- Local SEO is included when relevant.
- AIO guidance improves clarity without keyword stuffing.

## Security and Ethics

- Do not hide misleading claims for ranking purposes.
- Do not recommend spammy links or deceptive structured content.
- Do not expose private analytics exports in public outputs.
